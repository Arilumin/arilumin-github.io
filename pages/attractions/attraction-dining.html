Dining and Grocery

Dining

If you are looking for food, Taniti is the place to be! We have 5 unique restaurants that serve locally caught and grown fish and rice dishes, the Taniti specialties!


However, if you are not interested in the local cuisine, fret not because there are 3 American style restaurants and 2 Pan-Asian restaurants within Taniti City. Whatever you are craving, there are tons of options!

Grocery

If you don't wish to spend a pretty penny on somebody cooking for you, Taniti also offers grocery options. There are two supermarkets open daily that provide not only food but also any supplies you may have left behind at home. This island also has two smaller grocery stores that provide extremely quality local foods.


If it gets late and the markets close, don't fret! We also have a convenience store that is open 24/7. So if you need anything in the middle of the night, we've got you covered!
