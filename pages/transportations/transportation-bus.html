Bus Information

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti city and run from 5am to 11pm daily. They are $1.50 for each ride or you can purchase a daily pass for $5.


Private Buses

Private buses serve the rest of the island. They tend to be a bit more expensive and you have to reserve a seat ahead of time. Call 123-456-7890 for scheduling services!
